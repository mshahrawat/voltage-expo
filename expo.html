About the Expo

The purpose of the Expo, co sponsored by Voltage and the EECS Department, is to bring together and highlight opportunities in electrical engineering for current MIT freshmen and other undergraduates in EECS.

What's going on?

The day will be dedicating to displaying all the opportunities in electrical engineering. The Expo is split up into three parts: faculty presentations, company presentations, and a networking fair.

View Full Schedule »

Where and When?

The Expo is Friday, October 16, 2015. Be sure to mark the date! It takes place on the MIT campus in the RLE Allen & Haus Room (Rooms 36-462 and 36-428). The street address is 50 Vassar Street.
