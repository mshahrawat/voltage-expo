Voltage

MIT IEEE/ACM's Electrical Engineering Subcommittee

V = IR?

Voltage is the electrical engineering subcommittee of MIT's IEEE/ACM chapter. We are a group of undergraduates working to create a stronger, more cohesive MIT undergraduate EE community by organizing events that allow students, faculty, alumni, and industry to interact. Basically, we make being Course 6-1 even more awesome. Some events we have held in the past (and will definitely hold again so make sure to add yourself to the mailing list) are the Research Expo, Alumni Dinner, and random study breaks and dinners.
